Flex Container with 3 Items
[1] [2] [3]  ← items
1. space-around (what you asked)
  [1]     [2]     [3]

Each item has equal space around it.

Space at the edges (before 1 and after 3) is half the space between items.

2. space-between
[1]         [2]         [3]

No space at the edges, only between items.

3. space-evenly
   [1]      [2]      [3]

Equal space everywhere, including edges.

4. flex-start
[1][2][3]

Items stick to the start/left.

5. center
   [1][2][3]

Items are centered, with equal space on the left and right.
